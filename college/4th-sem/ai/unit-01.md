# 1. Introduction

The **History of AI** describes how Artificial Intelligence developed over time.

AI started with idea:  
**Can machines think like humans?**

Over time, scientists developed:

- Intelligent programs
    
- Search algorithms
    
- Expert systems
    
- Machine learning
    
- Modern AI
    

---

# 2. Timeline of AI History (Very Important)

Learn in order — exam favorite

---

## 1. 1943 – First AI Concept

**McCulloch & Pitts** proposed first **artificial neuron model**

Importance:

- Beginning of neural networks
    
- Brain inspired computing
    

---

## 2. 1950 – Turing Test

**Alan Turing** proposed **Turing Test**

Idea:  
If machine can talk like human → intelligent

This was first formal AI idea

Very important for exam

---

## 3. 1956 – Birth of AI (Most Important)

**Dartmouth Conference** held in 1956

John McCarthy:

- Coined term **Artificial Intelligence**
    
- AI became research field
    

This is considered:  
⭐ Birth of Artificial Intelligence

---

## 4. 1960s – Early AI Programs

Researchers built:

- Problem solving programs
    
- Game playing systems
    
- Theorem proving
    

Examples:

- Chess program
    
- Logic solver
    

AI progress started

---

## 5. 1970s – First AI Winter

AI progress slowed because:

- Computers slow
    
- High expectations
    
- Poor results
    

Funding stopped

This period called:  
**AI Winter**

Important exam term

---

## 6. 1980s – Expert Systems

AI became popular again

Expert systems:

- Used knowledge base
    
- Used rules
    
- Made decisions
    

Example:  
Medical diagnosis system

AI used in industry

---

## 7. Late 1980s – Second AI Winter

Again AI slowed due to:

- Expensive systems
    
- Hard maintenance
    
- Limited capability
    

Funding reduced again

Second AI Winter

---

## 8. 1990s – Machine Learning Era

AI shifted to:

- Learning from data
    
- Statistical methods
    
- Neural networks
    

Important milestone:  
Computer defeated human in chess

AI improved

---

## 9. 2000s – Modern AI

AI development in:

- Robotics
    
- Speech recognition
    
- Computer vision
    
- NLP
    

Internet + big data helped

---

## 10. Present – Deep Learning & AI Boom

Modern AI includes:

- Self driving cars
    
- Chatbots
    
- Image recognition
    
- Voice assistants
    

AI now widely used
